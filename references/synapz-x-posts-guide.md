## Overview

This guide enables AI agents to create engaging X posts for Synapz that align with our brand voice, project portfolio, and content strategy. Posts should be short, pitchy, and concise while maintaining our sophisticated yet accessible tone.

## Content Strategy

### Post Frequency
- **Daily**: 2 posts per day
- **Weekly**: 2 longer-form posts (blog content)
- **Total**: ~14 posts per week

### Content Mix

## Post Templates

### 1. Technical Achievement Post
**Structure**: Achievement + Context + Implication

**Template**: "[Project] [achievement]. [Context that explains significance]."

**Example**: "Templar SN3 processes 90B+ tokens daily. Live proof that decentralized AI training works at scale."

### 2. Analytical Take Post
**Structure**: Observation + Analysis + Open Question

**Template**: "[Observation about development]. [Analysis of what it means]. [Open question worth considering]."

**Example**: "Decentralized training is now processing billions of tokens daily. The technical barriers are falling. The interesting question is what happens to AI development incentives when anyone can contribute compute."

### 3. Project Update Post
**Structure**: Update + Context + Significance

**Template**: "[Project] [update]. [Context and significance]."

**Example**: "Rayon Labs launches Squad agent builder. The bridge between Bittensor's decentralized intelligence and practical deployment. Could unlock a new era of composable AI."

### 4. Industry Commentary Post
**Structure**: Observation + Analysis + Implication

**Template**: "Watching [trend/development]. [Analysis]. [Implication]."

**Example**: "Watching the AI agent ecosystem evolve. Centralized APIs are the bottleneck. Decentralized coordination is the future."

### 5. Blog Promotion Post
**Structure**: Hook + Value + Link

**Template**: "[Compelling hook from blog post]. [Key insight]. Read the full analysis: [link]"

**Example**: "In an era of AI hype, what if the real revolution is happening in decentralized training? Our latest analysis explores how Bittensor is reshaping the AI landscape. Read more: [link]"
